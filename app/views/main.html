Attendance Made Simple

Simple

  • Check in students with a single tap.
  • Add students on the iPad or import from a CSV file.
  • See who is present at a glance.
  • Portrait and landscape orientations supported.
  • Display on a projector or TV via Apple TV.

Student Details

  • Basic info such as name, email, phone number.
  • Track student allergies and medications.
  • Notes field for capturing any additional types of information.

Care Takers

  • Add information about student's care takers.
  • Select if care taker is approved for student pick-up.
  • Track who picked up each student on check out.

Reporting

  • Class, student, and date based reporting.
  • Set custom date ranges for each report.
  • Export student information.
  • Reports are generated in CSV format and can be printed to an AirPrint compatible printer.

Communications

  • Easily email all students and/or care takers from the class screen.
  • Email weekly attendance to a student and/or their care taker(s) from the student detail screen.
  • Send class reports.
